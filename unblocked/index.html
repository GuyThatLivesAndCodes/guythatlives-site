Recent Outages & Fixes Explained

March 12, 2026

What happened? Many of you experienced outages, errors, and the dreaded "too many redirects" error over the past 12-24 hours. We want to be transparent about what caused these issues and how we've resolved them.

The root cause was a SSL/TLS encryption misconfiguration. In an attempt to fix recent issues, our encryption was set to dynamic mode when it should have been set to full encryption. This mismatch caused a cascade of problems including redirect loops, connection errors, and games failing to load.

Good news! We've corrected the SSL/TLS settings, and approximately 250 games now work perfectly on the site without requiring you to visit the source directly. The gaming experience should be significantly smoother.

If you encounter any errors or issues with specific games, please use our report system to let us know. Your feedback helps us keep improving the platform for everyone.
