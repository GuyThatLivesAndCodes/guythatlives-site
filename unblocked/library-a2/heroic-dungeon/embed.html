Take your character through a series of dungeons and fight the evil monsters! This is dungeon questing with a twist. Each dungeon can be explored - simply click on a tile to discover what lies there. Maybe its a monster? Maybe its a health boost or extra attack damage? Find the key to unlock the next dungeon, and fight monsters on your way. To attack a monster, you must complete a puzzle game. Each puzzle board presents you with a series of monsters. You must match 3 or more of the same monster in a row to build up your attack power. Look out for special items that clear multiple monsters at once! Aside from the gameplay, you can also upgrade your character - improve their armor and weaponry to make them stronger. How fall will you progress through the dungeons?
