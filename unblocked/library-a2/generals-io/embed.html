Generals.io is a highly tactical strategy game from the ever popular .io games series. In Generals.io you take command of a General square. From there, you must move from square to square, recruiting armies and gaining in numbers and strength; moving about the map until you find, capture and control the opposing general’s tile. Choose your moves wisely as the opponent is looking to capture your general as well! Do you have what it take to capture the enemy general or will you and your armies be defeated?
