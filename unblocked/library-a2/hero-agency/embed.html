Hero Agency is a adventure incremental game. Play as the son of a great adventurer, a great man and loved by all. He was considered a legendary adventurer. He also accumulated a fortune during his lifetime. After his death, as his son, you can’t quite live up to the same physical abilities and fortitude as your great father. So to keep the family business going, you used your inheritance money to start a Hero Agency, where the goal is to gain fame and fortune by hiring talented individuals who can adventuring business forward to the future.
