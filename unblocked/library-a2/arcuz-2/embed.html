Arcuz 2 is an addictive action role-playing game inspired by Zelda and Diablo. Your mission is simple: Explore the dungeons of Arcuz, fight monsters and save the world. Time to begin your adventure now. Go Go Go!
Controls: WASD = Move, J = Attack, K = Jump, U/I/O/L = Spells, 1/2/3 = Use potions/town portals, C = Characters, B = Inventory, V = Skills, N = Quest, M = Map
