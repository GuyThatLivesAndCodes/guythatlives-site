Are you bored with playing games where the stages are premade for you? well in free Rider, you are making the levels! Use the tools to draw out the path to your liking, when you have completed your masterpiece, leap onto your bike and attempt to complete the level! you're in control of how hard the level is, you may make the game challenging or you may make it easy. This wouldn’t be a motorcycle game if you couldn’t do cool tricks and flips, so as you experience through your level, bear in mind to show off and pull off a few stunts!
