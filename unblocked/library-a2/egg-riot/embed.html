Egg Riot is an awe-inspiring action game in which your mission is to crush eggs to prevent them from passing. You can catch an egg and smash it or throw to its friends to cause multiple damages. Each round has a special object that you simply will use, such as a heater or a slingshot. You can equip up to four power-ups in every round. Score higher and earn additional stars to unlock more power-ups. If you're overcome, throw the eggs further!
