A fan made fnaf point-and-click survival game. Survive each night as a security guard working the night shift. But beware of evil toy animatronics. Switch between web cams to see everything that is going on. Animatronics will not move if they are being watched. But when you switch to another cam feed who knows what they’ll do. As Always, Good Luck and Have Fun. Instruction: The game has no proper loading/downloading screen, it will just stay a white screen until it has loaded. The whole file is 52.8 MB so if you have decent internet it should not be an issue. Yes this game does contain an extras menu at the end with cheats and even the custom night.
