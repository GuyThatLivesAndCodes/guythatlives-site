The great an powerful King Wilhelm declares a mission to locate the Holy Grail, which was lost by his grandfather. Rumours say that the ancient chalice has been guarded by a great dragon, the most powerful creature to ever live! The person who finds it shall marry the King's beautiful daughter. So, it's time for the brave knight Richard to uncover the Holy Grail! Make your way to the dragon's lair fighting enemies and using various tactics on the battlefield. Can you overcome all the obstacles, conquer the dark forces and claim the King's prize in the end?
