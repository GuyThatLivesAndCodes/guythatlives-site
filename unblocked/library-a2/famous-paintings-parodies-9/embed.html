Have fun with this sequel to Famous Paintings Parodies 8! You have to guess the correct painting names in this game. That won't be easy because the paintings are drawn in a cartoon/illustration style. The paintings are from famous painters, such as Pablo Picasso, Da Vinci, and Van Gogh. Compare with the real painting by hovering your mouse over the eye icon to get a better idea. Get a medal by answering correctly. Are you an art lover? Good luck! Left mouse button to choose an answer.
