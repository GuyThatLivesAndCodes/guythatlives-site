Asgard Skill Master is a highly addicting, real time action game that puts you in the role of the Skill Master as you try to save what remains of Asgard. Progress through the tiers of enemies and earn gold, gems and find treasure chests and use your loot to upgrade your allies. Unlock several different allies, to fight alongside of you and defeat the bosses that stand in your way! Do you have what it takes to save Asgard?
