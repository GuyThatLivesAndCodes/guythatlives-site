Dogs versus Homework is a clicker game about outsourcing your homework to your canine friends ! Bunches of homework is waiting for you , however you are excessively sluggish, making it impossible to do it . Why not let your canines do it for you ? They have their own one of a kind approach to do it ! Procure enough A's to open the primary pooch . Open new pooch breeds to help you produce significantly more A's . Adorn your stay with different enhancements that join your new canine buys . At the later stages , you can send even send your pooches to go to classes ! Have a ton of fun
