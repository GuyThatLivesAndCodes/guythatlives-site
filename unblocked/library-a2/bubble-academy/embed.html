Bubble Academy Unblocked is a match-3 bubble shooter game. Shoot the colored bubbles and stick them together with at least 2 other bubbles of the same color to make them pop. If you are stuck, you can switch the bubble color with the reserve. Pay attention to the stock you have! Try to pop the special bubble to let the surroundings explode. Can you collect all 3 stars in each level?
Game controls
To shoot the bubble press left click
To switch the bubble, use left click or right click on the reserve
