Don't Tax Me, Bro is an endless running game. You are a successful businessman who decided not to pay taxes! Cops are coming to your house to arrest you, so you have to run away. Jump over obstacles that can slow you down and avoid getting hit by a falling pot or falling into a hole. Hire bodyguards along the way to help you fight the cops when they come near you and collect other power-ups. Collect coins and try to run as far as you can. Have fun running for your life and your money!
