Begin your quest for domination by clicking on the mounds of freshly dug graves. Keep clicking until you have enough bones for more plots! Now you’ve got the hang of it.
Summon minions to do the dirty work for you, upgrade them to work harder and faster, delve deep into the catacombs and other spooky activities. Once you’ve gathered a massive mound of bones, turn them into troops for the skeleton horde and do it all over again.
