Drive over obstacles, jump off the stunt ramps, collect coins, and try to stay ahead of your mysterious followers. In Absotruckinlutely, a new side-scrolling truck driving game, you control a fully customizable monster truck and need to overcome any obstacles along your way! You are free to do crazy stunts in the race, including long wheelies or backflips! Collect money along the way and increase it by performing stunts. Customize your truck with bigger parts and beat the challenges that are waiting for you.
