Utilizing the WASD keyboard keys, you need to direct our magician through a string of varying difficult levels. Through each level you have to first complete a variety of challenges to be able to proceed. You must jump on and over a range of moving and stationary platforms that may sound simple but it's truly rather challenging! Some platforms go down or up when you plan them. Other programs move left or right. Furthermore, some openings can only be crossed by throwing your magic hat. If you see a stone statue -- you must throw your jacket onto it and then teleport into the statue. It becomes increasingly difficult as soon as the figurines and platforms are still moving! Hat Wizard is refreshing, distinctive and hard so wear your wizards hat today!
