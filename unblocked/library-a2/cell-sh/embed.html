Cell.sh is a fun multiplayer survival game that is very similar to Agar.io. within the game you control a cell and should eat little dots or smaller players to gain mass. you'll be able to get within an endemic (green circle) to cover from different players. But beware, once your mass is larger then your cell goes to separate. once following different players, feed your cell forward. you'll be able to additionally play in several game modes. do not forget to log in with Facebook or Google account to customize your cell's avatar. however massive are you able to grow? Have fun!
