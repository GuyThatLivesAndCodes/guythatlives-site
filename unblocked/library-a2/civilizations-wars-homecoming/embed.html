Civilizations Wars: Homecoming is a strategy game set in ancient times. You choose one of 3 different clans and must face other clans in territorial battles. In battle, try to occupy empty buildings first to gain a speed advantage when deploying troops. The tower is an essential building that must be captured to produce stronger units and act as a defensive building. There are several challenges that can be chosen on each level to give more bonus. Occupy all enemy's buildings to win the level. Don't forget to upgrade skills with your earned points.
