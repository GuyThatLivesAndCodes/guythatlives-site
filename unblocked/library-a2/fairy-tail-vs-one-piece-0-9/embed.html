Fairy Tail Vs One Piece 0.9 – There are four different modes including a training mode in which you can play this game. You will be controlling your chosen character to fight against the oppositions to defeat them one by one.
Menu Screen:
Z/S: Up/Down
A/D: Left/Right
J: Select


Game Controls:
A/D: Left/Right
S: Defense
J: Attack
U: Distance attack
K: Jump
L: Dash
I: Special attack
