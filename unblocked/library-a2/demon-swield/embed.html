Demon Swield is a great action game where you control a hero in a dark realm. With only a sword in your left hand and a shield in your right hand, you must defeat all the demons that await. Try to attack as fast as possible and block the enemy attack by raising your shield. If you successfully defend an enemy attack, you can build flow that will multiply your damage. You will get new equipment after each chapter. The game will restart when you are defeated, but this also levels you up. Press A or left arrow to swing your sword and D or right arrow to raise your shield.
