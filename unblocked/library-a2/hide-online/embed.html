Hide Online is a multiplayer game with a very unique game play. This game consists of two teams, the Props and the Hunters. The Props are the ones who transforms to objects. They hide and taunt just to confuse the Hunters. The only objective of the Hunters is to shoot the Props. In this game the Pros have only 30 sec to hide by transforming to any object they wish and after that the next 30 second they will taunt or make a sound and it's up to the Hunters to look for them. Just remember not to shoot the wrong object or else you'll lose some life points. You have got a couple of minutes to search and kill the Props or else they will win the game.It's a game of hide and seek with a twist!
