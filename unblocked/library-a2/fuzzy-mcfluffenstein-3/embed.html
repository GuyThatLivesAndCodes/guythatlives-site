Jump on the mice to make them explode for more points and time. Beware of the rocket powered mice and shooting hamsters as they will try to kill fuzzy mcfluffenstein 3 game. The object of fuzzy mcfluffenstein 3 game is to jump on top of as many mice as you can to make the mice explode. Your will gain more playing time and points will be awarded. If you don't explode enough mice or your time will run out and the fuzzy mcfluffenstein 3 game will end. This version has rocket powered flying mice and hamsters that shoot at you, you must avoid these.
