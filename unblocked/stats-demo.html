📚 How It Works

Session Tracking: When you load this page, a unique session is created in Firebase

Heartbeat: Every 30 seconds, your session sends a heartbeat to stay "online"

Game Tracking: When you "play" a game, your session is tagged with that game ID

Statistics: The dashboard queries all active sessions and aggregates the data

Cleanup: Sessions without heartbeats for 90+ seconds are removed automatically
