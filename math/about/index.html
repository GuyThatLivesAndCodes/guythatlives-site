Who Is This For?

Homeschool Parents

Create your own private school completely free. Track multiple students, assign lessons, and monitor progress all in one place. No subscriptions, no hidden fees.

Public Schools

Large institutions can create public schools with multiple classrooms, teachers, and hundreds of students. After Host Manager approval, your school will be listed publicly, helping other educators discover and learn from your setup.

Teachers & Educators

Manage your classroom periods, assign specific courses, and track individual student progress. Our platform adapts to your teaching style and curriculum needs.

Individual Students

Practice at your own pace with unlimited problems. Our mistake detection helps you learn from errors, not just repeat them. Track your progress and earn achievements as you master each concept.
